Professional Experiences

Internship at MSK Technologies (5 Months)

During my final year at university, I completed a five-month internship at MSK Technologies, where I worked on developing MSK Academy, an innovative e-learning platform. The platform integrates gamification elements to enhance user engagement and make learning interactive.

This project, which also served as my final year project, was built using:

  • Frontend: Angular
  • Backend: .NET
  • Database: SQL Server

Following the Scrum methodology, I ensured efficient task management and seamless collaboration. This experience strengthened my expertise in full-stack development and underscored the importance of creating scalable, user-friendly applications.

Summer Internship at OMMP

  • Collaborated closely with teams, developing problem-solving and analytical skills.

This experience broadened my understanding of how technology can enhance operational efficiency in diverse industries.
